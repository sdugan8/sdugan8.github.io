This is a tic tac toe game made in c# by using visual studio. It's a simple game that can be played by your self or with a friend both single player and two player function relativly the same, one goes after the other till someone wins or a draw. The Ai moves by picking a square at random that hasnt been taken already. In two player above the board a text field keeps track who turn it is to go. The game finds a winner by giving a value to each spot 1 for the first player and -1 for the second. after every turn the game adds the values of the spots that can make a three in a row. If its postive 3 player 1 wins, negative 3 player 2 wins. If it is neither then the game continue's unless all spots are taken an conciders it a draw.
